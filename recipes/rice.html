Fried Rice

Nigerian Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 3 tbsp oil for frying
  • 1 cup onion diced
  • 1 cup mixed Vegetables Carrots, Sweet Peas, Sweet Corn and green beans
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp Curry powder
  • 2 scallions diced
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 1 cup beef liver cubed
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Add the boiled rice to the stock in a medium pot and cook on medium heat until the water is dried up.
  2. Preheat the oil in a pan on medium to high heat, throw in the onions, and fry for a minute or two;
  3. add the scallions, mixed vegetables, thyme, curry powder, salt, and stock Cube.

  5. Take it off the heat and serve.
