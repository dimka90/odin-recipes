Indomie noodles

Indomie noodles, tasty nutrition, good for you..!

Ingredients

  • Maggi
  • Egg
  • Seasoning
  • Carrot
  • Greenbeans
  • indomie noodles

Steps

  1. Set a pot on heat, add two cups of water and allow to boil.
  2. Dice the carrots and chop the green bean
  3. Boil eggs separately, deshell and set aside.
  4. In the pot with boiling water, combine noodles, carrots, and green beans
  5. Preferred meat source (Eggs or Chicken are the most widely used).
  6. Allow cooking for 3 minutes, add the follow come spices, allow another minute.
