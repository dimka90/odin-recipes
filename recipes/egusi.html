Egusi is super delicous

Taste like no other

Ingredients

  • Ground melon
  • smoked turkey
  • Smoked Mackerel
  • salt
  • Beef stock cubes
  • Nigerian pepper mix
  • Dried Prawns
  • Palm oil
  • Spinach: any type of spinach can be used but I used baby spinach

Steps

  1. Heat palm oil on fire for one minute den add di Une( iru)
  2. Add stock small-small, den set di fire on low heat make e cook small.
  3. Add teaspoon size of di egusi paste mixture to di stock. Make sure say e form ball.
  4. Leave am to cook for 20-30 minutes so di egusi balls go cook well-well.
  5. Add di meat and fish and oda orisirisi.
  6. Add di pumpkin leaves and waterleaf
  7. Turn am and cover di pot, den leave am to cook for 7-10 minutes till di leaves don soft.
  8. Add di bitterleaf. No cover di pot again until e don cook finish for anoda 5-10 minutes.
  9. Turn am, check weda seasoning dey inside and evritin dey taste ok.
